About C'Groovy

C'Groovy is a music streaming platform that offers a new color and nuance of listening experience. We believe that music is not only entertainment, but also a form of expression, inspiration, and emotional connection.


Our Mission

Providing easy and affordable access to music from a variety of genres and artists, supporting local and global creativity.

Our Vision

To be the best music streaming platform that unites listeners and musicians from all over the world.

Our Journey


2016

C’Groovy is a renowned music streaming service established in 2016. It offers a vast library of music from diverse genres and artists across the world. With a mission to bring music to everyone’s fingertips, C’Groovy allows users to discover, stream, and share songs that resonate with them, anytime and anywhere.



2020

Leveraging the latest technology, C’Groovy start to grow even more. In 2020, C'Groovy had been downloaded for 1 millions making C'Groovy enter the worldclass music streaming platforms.



2024

With the rapid growth of information technology in 2024, C’Groovy plans to dive into the world of digital business. C’Groovy create a company website that represents C’Groovy’s image. With this website's existence, we hope that we can improve our service towards customers.
